Model E


Mercedes E-Class is a series of luxury business class cars produced since 1984. The letter E in the name came from the word Einspritzung which means "fuel squirt". This letter has been used since the 1960s to distinguish cars that were equipped with a fuel injection system, which was new at that time. Then all of the company's cars began to use the fuel injection system, and there was no longer a need to use a special symbol to distinguish it.
